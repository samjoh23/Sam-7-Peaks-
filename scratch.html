Seven Summits and Their First Ascents

  1. Asia: Mount Everest (8,848m)
    • First ascent: Edmund Hillary and Tenzing Norgay (1953)
  2. South America: Aconcagua (6,961m)
    • First ascent: Matthias Zurbriggen (1897)
  3. North America: Denali (6,190m)
    • First ascent: Hudson Stuck, Harry Karstens, Walter Harper, Robert Tatum (1913)
  4. Africa: Kilimanjaro (5,895m)
    • First ascent: Hans Meyer and Ludwig Purtscheller (1889)
  5. Europe: Mount Elbrus (5,642m)
    • First ascent: Khillar Khachirov (1829)
  6. Antarctica: Mount Vinson (4,897m)
    • First ascent: Nicholas Clinch-led expedition (1966)
  7. Oceania: Puncak Jaya/Carstensz Pyramid (4,884m)
    • First ascent: Heinrich Harrer et al. (1962)
